Progression / meal plan (what you cook + how you serve)

Menu

Shorshe Ilish (Hilsa in mustard sauce) — main event

Kalijeera rice — base

Aloo Bhorta (mustard-oil mashed potato) — essential side

Simple Bengali salad (cucumber + tomato + onion + lemon) — freshness

Mishti doi — dessert
(+ achar on the table if you bought it)

Serving flow

Start: plates out with salad + achar (people nibble while you plate)

Main: rice first, then ilish on top, then spoon mustard gravy around

Side: aloo bhorta in its own bowl (people add as they go)

Finish: mishti doi straight from the fridge

Execution timeline (same-day cooking)

T-24h (optional but best): thaw hilsa in fridge

T-90 min: set rice + potatoes; prep mustard paste + salad mise en place

T-35 min: start rice + boil potatoes

T-25 min: cook shorshe ilish

T-10 min: mash bhorta; toss salad

T-0: plate and serve
